Licensed Surveying

Precision land surveying & legal documentation

Accurate land surveying is the foundation of successful construction. Our licensed surveyors use advanced equipment including Total Stations and GPS technology to provide precise boundary demarcation, contour mapping, and survey documentation that meets all government requirements for DTCP, CMDA, and municipal approvals.

Equipment

Total Station, DGPS, Auto Level & Drone Mapping

Deliverables

Survey drawings, FMB sketch, coordinates & reports

Turnaround

2-5 days for residential, 1-2 weeks for large plots

Surveying Services

  • Boundary survey and demarcation with permanent markers
  • Topographic survey with contour mapping
  • Construction layout and setting out
  • As-built survey for completed structures
  • Subdivision survey for plot division
  • Road alignment and utility surveys
  • Volume calculations for earthwork

Survey Process

  • Step 1: Document collection - patta, FMB, sale deed review
  • Step 2: Site reconnaissance and control point establishment
  • Step 3: Field survey with Total Station/GPS equipment
  • Step 4: Data processing and coordinate calculations
  • Step 5: AutoCAD drawing preparation with all details
  • Step 6: Report submission with certified survey plan

Key Benefits

  • Government-approved licensed surveyor certificates
  • High accuracy with sub-centimeter precision
  • Digital deliverables in AutoCAD DWG and PDF formats
  • Coordination with revenue department for FMB verification
  • Legal validity for property transactions and disputes
  • Quick turnaround without compromising accuracy

Why Choose Our Survey Services?

  • Licensed surveyors registered with government authorities
  • Modern equipment ensuring highest accuracy standards
  • Experience with DTCP, CMDA, and municipal submissions
  • Comprehensive documentation for legal purposes
  • Competitive pricing with transparent quotations
  • On-site pegging and boundary marking included
